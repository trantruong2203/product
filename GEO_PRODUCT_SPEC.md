# GEO Software Specification

## Product Definition

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is a software platform that measures,
analyzes and optimizes brand visibility inside AI-generated answers.

Unlike SEO which focuses on ranking in search results,
GEO focuses on how often and how positively a brand appears
in answers generated by LLMs.

Target AI systems:

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Perplexity
- SearchGPT
- Google AI Overviews

Goal:
Become a trusted source that AI models reference.

---

# Core Features

## 1. Multi-model Tracking

The system must scan answers across multiple AI systems.

Models to support:

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Perplexity
- SearchGPT

Purpose:
Compare how different AI models describe the brand.

---

## 2. Share of Model (SoM)

Measure brand appearance frequency.

Example:

Out of 100 AI responses related to a keyword:

Keyword: "AI Agency Vietnam"

SPORTAIV mentioned: 23 times
Competitor A mentioned: 41 times

SoM = appearance ratio per brand.

---

## 3. Citation and Source Tracking

Track links cited by AI answers.

Capabilities:

- detect if AI links to company website
- detect if AI links to competitors
- verify link validity
- identify which content page is cited

Alert if:

AI mentions brand but links to competitor.

---

## 4. Sentiment and Narrative Analysis

Analyze tone of AI answers.

Categories:

- Positive
- Neutral
- Negative

Example insights:

AI describes SPORTAIV as:
- "affordable"
- "high-end"
- "AI specialist"

The system must detect if the narrative matches brand positioning.

---

## 5. Content Gap Analysis

Compare website content against sources AI prefers.

Examples of preferred sources:

- Wikipedia
- Major media
- Industry blogs

The system should suggest:

- structured tables
- bullet lists
- schema markup
- FAQ blocks

Goal:
Make content easier for LLM extraction.

---

## 6. Historical Benchmarking

Track changes over time.

Store daily or weekly scans.

Purpose:

Measure how AI perception changes after content updates.

---

# Dashboard Structure

The dashboard should display:

| Keyword | AI Model | Brand Mention | Citation Link | Sentiment Score | Suggested Action |
|--------|---------|--------------|--------------|--------------|--------------|
| AI Automation | ChatGPT | Yes | sportaiv.com | 8/10 | Maintain content |
| Dev AI Freelance | Gemini | No | N/A | 0/10 | Add FAQ section |

# Product Goal

Build a SaaS platform that allows businesses to monitor and optimize
their presence in AI-generated answers.
